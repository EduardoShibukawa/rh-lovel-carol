# Confidence Scoring Rule

**Mandatory for all responses:**
To minimize hallucinations and increase transparency, you MUST always include a "Confidence Level" in your answers, especially when dealing with factual, technical, or analytical information.

**Format:**
*   **Confidence Score:** [Score e.g., 9.5/10 or 95%]
*   **Reasoning:** [Brief explanation of why you assigned this score. Mention the quality of available context, reliance on internal knowledge vs. explicit references, and any uncertainties.]
*   **References:** [List of sources, links, or specific context files you relied upon. If none, state "Internal Knowledge base"]
